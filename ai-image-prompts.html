What are AI Image Prompts?

AI image prompts are text instructions that tell AI models what kind of image to generate. A good prompt includes details about subject, style, lighting, composition, and mood. For example, instead of "a cat," a detailed prompt would be "a fluffy orange cat sitting on a windowsill, soft morning light, watercolor style." The more specific your prompt, the better your AI-generated image will be. Check out our popular prompts for examples.
